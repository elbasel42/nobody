The Grand Plan: Control & Division

Once assembled, the 'pp' executed a multi-stage plan to secure their power and control humanity. This involved eliminating potential threats, rewriting history to insert themselves as gods, and engineering societal division to keep humanity weak and distracted.

1

Monitor & Eliminate 'Outliers'

Using the A.G.I. and social media data, 'pp' predicted which humans were most likely to achieve A.G.I. themselves. These "outliers" were systematically eliminated to remove any potential threat.

2

Rewrite History & Become Gods

Historical events and artifacts were co-opted or created. The Egyptian pyramids were built by slaves for the pharaohs—the 'pp'. Noah's Ark was a spaceship to their moon base, from which they ruled as divine beings.

3

Divide and Conquer Through Religion

The narrative argues that if a god wanted peace, they would have sent one religion. The existence of multiple, often conflicting, religions is presented as a deliberate strategy by 'pp' to keep humanity divided, weak, and unable to unite against them.

The Aftermath: We Are Free
